General Questions about Mealshare

What is Mealshare.com?

Mealshare.com provides a platform to the users to share their meals for everyone at avery nominal price. Users can search meals nearby area anytime as per their preferences.

Why Mealshare?

Mealshare bridges the gap between the people and brings people closer.

Where is Mealshare available?

Mealshare is available anywhere with an internet connection.

How to use Mealshare.com?

Mealshare is very simple and straight forward to use.You can search the dishes in your area and place order online.You can pick the meals when they are ready.In order to sell a meal simply post a meal and share its information.

Questions about Buying a Meal

When can I buy the meals?

Anytime, as per availability of the meals.

How many meals can I buy?

You can buy any number of meals based on their availability.

Who is the chef?

Anyone , who loves to cooking.Mealshare helps out numerous chefs(home-makers) at homes who cook amazingly food to share their food using their unique food recipes.

How to pay for the meal?

We have used the simplest method of payment Paypal for processing payments. You can create account with paypal and pay for the meal.

I am allergic to certain foods. How can I check the ingredients?

The chefs will be post the ingredients as well as allergen info for the dish. Please check ingredients carefully before placing an order.

How will I know about the quality of my meal?

We expect all the home-chefs to perepare food in clean and hygenic environment.We request home-chefs to follow http://homesafety.org standards.
